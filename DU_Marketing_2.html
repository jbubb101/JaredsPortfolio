AI Tool For DU Marketing

Duration: 3 months

Completed: October 2024

Role: AI Engineer

Tech Stack: Python, Amazon Web Services (AWS), OpenAI API, Computer Vision

Project Demo

Demo video showcasing the DU Marketing Tool's key features and functionality.

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to build a custom AI tool for the Daniels Office of Communications and Marketing (OCM) to eliminate repetitive tasks while improving the accuracy and quality of deliverables. The AI assistant, Wally, was developed to meet specific team requirements, including enhancing internal workflows, improving brand consistency, and automating content creation processes.

Key Objectives:

  • Automate monotonous, time-consuming tasks (e.g., caption generation, content analysis).
  • Improve the accuracy of marketing materials, adhering to the University of Denver style guide.
  • Use AI-powered visual tools to detect logo misuse and provide actionable insights.
  • Provide a customizable experience that grows with user needs over time.

Performance Metrics

Task Completion Time Improvement

  • 35% reduction in average task completion time across content creation and review processes.
  • Example: Internal newsletter submission copy edits reduced from 20 minutes to 5 minutes per task.

Accuracy Improvement

Error rates dropped by 45%, particularly for:

  • Formatting and stylistic issues in captions, blog posts, and social media content.
  • Logo misuse detection, ensuring visual assets align with DU branding guidelines.

The Daniel Marketing Tool is designed to streamline your workflow, whether you're generating captions and headers for media posts or using AI for logo detection. This tool enhances your efficiency by providing AI-powered analysis, offering suggestions and advice for text or emails in line with the University of Denver style guide.

As time passes, the AI will improve and become better suited to your needs, ultimately becoming a more effective product.

Methodology & Implementation

Stakeholder Requirements Gathering

Conducted interviews and workshops with the six-person OCM team to identify pain points, prioritize requirements, and define success metrics.

Key needs included:

  • Automation of copy editing and caption generation
  • Visual analysis for Daniels logo usage
  • AI-powered tools to enhance efficiency and ensure style compliance

Development and Design

Iterative development using an Agile methodology with bi-weekly feedback sessions.

Key technologies implemented:

  • Python: Backend logic and AI models
  • OpenAI API: Content analysis, generation, and suggestions
  • AWS: Scalability and deployment
  • Computer Vision: Logo detection and analysis

Testing and Feedback

  • Conducted comprehensive testing with real-world tasks provided by the OCM team
  • Iterative refinements based on user feedback to improve tool accuracy and usability

Development Timeline

  • Week 1-2: Stakeholder meetings, requirement gathering, and project scoping
  • Week 3-5: Initial development of AI-powered analysis and content tools
  • Week 6-8: Integration of computer vision for Daniels logo detection
  • Week 9-10: Implementation of customizable memory system and user testing
  • Week 11-12: Final refinements, bug fixes, and project handover

Impact & Results

The AI tool had a significant impact on the OCM team's efficiency and content quality:

  • Time Savings: Reduced repetitive tasks by an average of 65% across the team
  • Consistency: Ensured adherence to the Daniels and DU style guides, eliminating errors that previously required manual correction
  • Scalability: Enabled the team to focus on strategy and higher-level tasks, with Wally managing routine processes

By improving both accuracy and efficiency, this project not only addressed immediate team needs but also established a foundation for future AI integration within the Office of Communications and Marketing.
